The Best Movies According to Maithili

My top 3 movies of all time.

To All the Boys I've Loved Before

This movies was honestly really cute. At first, I thought she was gonna end up with her sister's boyfriend but I'm glad it was endgame with Peter.Also Kitty deserves all the rights for keeping Peter's notes. She wins the best sister award. I give this movie a 8/10.

Crazy Rich Asians

This movie is amazing!! It was really interesting seeing more Asian movies an seeing the culture and traditions instilled to the different generations. Absolutely love every scenes, the soundtrack, the costumes, just everything!!
Astrid has got to be one of my favourites! Her voice is soothing and calm!

The Holiday

My favourite Christmas movie! It was smart, funny, cute, full of love and good vibes.
